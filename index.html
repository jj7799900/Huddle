THE HUDDLE

Learn from others

What if connecting a coach in a daily, personalized, interactive way were as easy as heading to The Huddle?


Life has challenges. in a fast paced, tech savy world, the advice to "Grow" is often overlooked. the hard work of development is still vital. Guidance is still needed. Providing easily accessible, coaching content direct to your device, when you want it, how you want it, and for what guidance you need-- has never been easier.


WHAT WE DO

The Huddle gives coaches, teachers, leaders, and mentors a simple, interactive opertunity to connect with their tribe. By utilizing The Huddle's pre-written Coaching Series, or a Content Leaders own unique content uploaded into the app--Coaches can send Followers within their group a daily thouhght, challenge, inspiration, reading, video, question, or text based training. Followers have an opportunity to respond with "likes," answers to daily questions, or questions of their own-thus creating a daily conversational flow in a platform we thrive in- digital devices.


Recognizing that digital help is only the beginning-- The Huddle offers Coaches/Content Creators the ability to upload PDF based gathering outlines which coincide with each Coaching Series, allowing groups to meet in person and discuss in community what they are learning.
